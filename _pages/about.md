I am a graduate student in Brain Science at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University, Taiwan. I integrate my background in Kinesiology with expertise in cognitive neuroscience and statistical neuroimaging. My research focuses on clinical neuroscience which leverages fMRI/EEG to further the mechanism of neurodegenerative disease.

### **Research Interests**
* Cognitive Neuroscience
* Neuroimaging (fMRI, EEG)
* Exercise Psychology & Aging
